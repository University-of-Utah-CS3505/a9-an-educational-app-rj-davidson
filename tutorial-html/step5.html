Step 5: Create a Cross on the Opposite Face

Our next step is to create another cross, this time on the side opposite of our starting face. This side can have up to 4 permutations: a dot, an "L", a line, or a cross. We can use a short algorithm to go from one permutation to the next.

The algorithm we will use is pretty short. You will just have to repeat it until you get to the cross position.

Introduction

Make sure to read this to get a basic understanding of the components of the cube.

  • Fixed Center Pieces

    No matter what kind of face rotation you do, the center pieces always stay in the same position. They determine the color of each face.

  • Edges & Corners

    Besides the center pieces, the cube is made up of 8 corner pieces (a piece with 3 stickers on it) and 12 edge pieces (2 stickers).

  • Notation

    There are 6 total letters that define the cube in standard rubik's notation. These are as follows:

    • F (Front) - The face you are current looking at. All other faces are in reference to this face.
    • R (Right) - The face to the right hand side of the face you are currently looking at.
    • L (Left) - The face to the left hand side of the face you are currently looking at.
    • U (Up) - The face directly above the face you are currently looking at.
    • D (Down) - The face directly below the face you are currently looking at.
    • B (Back) - The face directly opposite of the face you are currently looking at.

    A letter by itself indicates a clockwise rotation. A letter with an apostraphe (F') indicates a counter clockwise turn. Double rotations are notated with a 2 after the letter (R2).

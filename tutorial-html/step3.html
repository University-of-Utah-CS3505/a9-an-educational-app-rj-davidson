Step 3: Solve the Corners

Our goal for this step is to move all the corner piece of our chosen color from the bottom middle of the cube to the top where our cross is located.

Helpful Algorithms

Red Sticker Facing Right

When the corner has a red sticker on the right face.

R' D' R

Red Sticker Facing Front

When the corner has a red sticker on front face.

F D F'

Red Sticker on Bottom

When the corner has a red sticker on the down face.

R' D2' R D R' D' R
